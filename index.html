State Policy Analysis Unit

Train students to think like policy analysts, not just memorize statistics.

Students enter a story-driven policy lab, review claims under pressure, identify weak evidence, and make defensible recommendations. Each week focuses on one major course concept through a public policy case.

How students use this
  • Select a week below.
  • Complete the narrative policy case.
  • Review the score summary screen.
  • Export or submit results at the end.
